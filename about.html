Our Story

About JJ Shop

A small Filipino business with a big mission — making professional-grade software accessible to every small business in the Philippines.

Who We Are

Born from a Real Business Problem

JJ Shop Solutions & Services was founded in 2026 after seeing firsthand how many small Filipino businesses were still running on paper records, basic Excel sheets, and manual processes — not because they didn't want better tools, but because professional software was too expensive or too complicated for them.

We set out to change that. Our goal is simple: build powerful, affordable, easy-to-use software specifically designed around how Philippine small businesses actually operate.

We understand the peso, the local business culture, the sari-sari store, the boutique, the small trading company — because we've worked with them and we are one of them.
