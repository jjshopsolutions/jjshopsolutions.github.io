Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need to have a technical background to work with you? ▾

Not at all! We handle all the technical details. All we need from you is a clear description of what your business needs. We'll translate that into a working software solution.

How do payments work for custom projects? ▾

We typically require a 50% downpayment before starting, with the remaining 50% due upon delivery. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based billing to reduce your risk.

Will I own the software once it's delivered? ▾

Yes. Once the final payment is made, the delivered software belongs to you. You receive the executable and we can discuss source code transfer on a case-by-case basis.

Do you provide support after the project is done? ▾

Yes! All projects include a 30-day free support period after delivery. Ongoing maintenance packages are also available at affordable monthly rates.

Can you work with my existing systems or software? ▾

Absolutely. We can integrate new solutions with your existing tools, migrate data from old systems, or build bridge solutions that connect different platforms together.
